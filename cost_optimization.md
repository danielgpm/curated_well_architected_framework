# AWS Well-Architected Framework: Cost Optimization Pillar

**Total Best Practices:** 7

---

## Practice cloud financial management

### Financial management

#### COST01-BP01: Establish a cost optimization function

**Description:** Create a team that is responsible for establishing and maintaining cost awareness across your organization.

**AWS Documentation:** [COST01-BP01](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/wellarchitected/latest/framework/cost_cloud_financial_management_function.html)

---

#### COST01-BP02: Establish a partnership between finance and technology

**Description:** Involve finance and technology teams in cost and usage discussions at all stages of your cloud journey.

**AWS Documentation:** [COST01-BP02](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/wellarchitected/latest/framework/cost_cloud_financial_management_partnership.html)

---

#### COST01-BP03: Establish cloud budgets and forecasts

**Description:** Adjust existing organizational budgeting and forecasting processes to be compatible with the highly elastic nature of cloud costs.

**AWS Documentation:** [COST01-BP03](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/wellarchitected/latest/framework/cost_cloud_financial_management_budget_forecast.html)

---

#### COST01-BP04: Implement cost awareness in your organizational processes

**Description:** Implement cost awareness into new or existing processes that impact usage, and leverage existing processes for cost awareness.

**AWS Documentation:** [COST01-BP04](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/wellarchitected/latest/framework/cost_cloud_financial_management_cost_awareness.html)

---

#### COST01-BP05: Report and notify on cost optimization

**Description:** Configure cost optimization reporting and notifications to provide insight into cost optimization efforts and achievements.

**AWS Documentation:** [COST01-BP05](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/wellarchitected/latest/framework/cost_cloud_financial_management_report_notify.html)

---

#### COST01-BP06: Monitor cost proactively

**Description:** Implement tooling and dashboards to monitor costs proactively for the workload. Do not just look at costs and categories at a monthly billing cycle.

**AWS Documentation:** [COST01-BP06](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/wellarchitected/latest/framework/cost_cloud_financial_management_proactive_monitoring.html)

---

#### COST01-BP07: Keep up to date with new service releases

**Description:** Regularly review AWS service releases and explore ways to optimize your workload with new services and features.

**AWS Documentation:** [COST01-BP07](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/wellarchitected/latest/framework/cost_cloud_financial_management_new_services.html)
